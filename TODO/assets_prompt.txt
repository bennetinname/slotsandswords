[STYLE]
16-bit pixel art, dark "dive-bar casino" dark-fantasy theme, grimy
neon-and-gold palette (deep purple #1a1228, gold #ffc640, red #dc3232,
teal accent #32dcdc), humorous-grotesque design, chunky readable
silhouette, bold 1px dark outline, limited ~32-color palette, dramatic
light from top-left, subtle dithering for shading, clean cel shading.

[TECH]
crisp pixels, nearest-neighbor, no anti-aliasing on outer edge,
export PNG with alpha, generate at 4x then downscale to target size.
